Observations for Just Egg & Just Meat Positioning

Market Landscape

  • Netherlands is the most developed market - 5 Beyond SKUs at AH, 6+ at Jumbo, Impossible entering. Dutch consumers have established plant-based purchasing behavior. NL should be the launch market for both Just Egg and Just Meat.
  • Price-per-ounce range: €0.51-0.88/oz - Beyond Burger at €0.59/oz is the anchor; specialty items (steak tips) command €0.88/oz. Impossible undercuts at €0.57/oz.
  • Beyond Meat pricing is identical across Dutch retailers - AH and Jumbo match on every overlapping SKU. No channel price competition.
  • Impossible is undercutting Beyond in NL - €0.57/oz vs €0.59/oz. Small per-oz gap, but pack price (€3.99 vs €4.69) creates stronger shelf perception.
  • Scandinavia is early-stage - 1-2 Beyond SKUs, no Impossible. Plant-based products from local brands (Wauw!, Hooked Foods, Stabburet), not US imports.
  • Norway's frozen format is cheapest per ounce - €0.57/oz (frozen) vs €0.59/oz (fresh NL) vs €0.61/oz (fresh Sweden). Frozen enables competitive pricing despite Norway's high tariffs.

Just Meat Positioning

  • Clear price gap to exploit - Beyond Burger is €4.69/226g in NL. Just Meat could enter at or below Impossible's €3.99/200g and claim the value position.
  • Plant-based chicken is wide open - No major US brand owns the PB chicken shelf in these markets. Valess (NL, vegetarian not vegan), Hooked Foods (Sweden, frozen), Wauw! (Norway, frozen). Beyond Chicken-Style is "voorlopig niet leverbaar" (temporarily unavailable) at Jumbo.
  • Jumbo is the beachhead retailer - Already carries Impossible (only Dutch retailer to do so), 23 PB products in search results, clearly open to new entrants. Redefine and Heüra also on shelf.
  • AH Terra private label is the floor - AH's own PB chicken (kipstukjes) starts at €1.99/160g. Any Just Meat chicken product needs to justify premium over store brands.

Just Egg Positioning

  • No direct competitor on shelf - Zero plant-based liquid egg products found at any retailer across all 3 markets. The Bridge VEGEGG (0.5L, 49.90 NOK) is the only vaguely adjacent product, and only at Oda Norway.
  • Category creation opportunity - Just Egg would define the category, not compete in one. Pricing should anchor to conventional egg prices (not PB meat prices).
  • NL shelf space is available - AH has deep vegan sections (5 Beyond SKUs, 6 PB chicken SKUs). Jumbo actively expands alternative protein. Both would likely allocate shelf for a novel category.
